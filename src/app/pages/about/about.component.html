About this app

One calm window onto Texas.

Texas City Snapshot gathers publicly available weather, traffic-camera, water-reservoir, and transportation data for every city and town across Texas, and lays it out in one quiet, fast place — no clutter, no accounts, no ads.

Where the data comes from

 Weather

Forecasts & history from Open-Meteo; alerts from the National Weather Service.

Updated every 5 minutes

Road cameras

Live snapshots from the Texas Department of Transportation.

Updated every 5 minutes

Water reservoirs

Reservoir levels from Water Data for Texas.

Updated every 24 hours

Population

City population figures sourced from the 2020 US Census.

Static dataset

Open data, gathered with care.
