About this app

Overview

Texas City Snapshot gathers publicly available weather data, road camera snapshots, and fullness of nearby reservoirs for any city or town in texas.

Where the data comes from

 Weather

Forecasts and history from Open-Meteo; alerts from the National Weather Service.

Updated every 5 minutes

Road cameras

Recent snapshots from the Texas Department of Transportation.

Updated every 5 minutes

Water reservoirs

Reservoir levels from Water Data for Texas.

Updated every 24 hours

Warning sirens

Siren locations sourced from the Texas Statewide Siren Map, a collaborative Google Map run by siren enthusiasts in Texas.

Updated every 24 hours

Electricity usage

Electricity usage numbers sourced from ERCOT via GridStatus.io.

Updated every 24 hours

Population

City population figures sourced from the 2020 US Census.

Questions you may have

What happened to the original app?

I took down the server after realizing there is a much more efficient way to wire everything up. Long story short, I was overpaying for hosting.

Why does the app look different now?

The deployment tool I'm using now (AWS Amplify) doesn't support the framework the original app was written in (Spring Boot). So the frontend has been rewritten in Angular.

Is this vibe coded?

Not completely, but I did have a good bit of help from AI.

Why Angular?

It was either going to be Angular or React, and I chose Angular because I haven't used it much before.
